Student Records

The Student Records feature provides a simple and organized way to manage student information in one place. It allows schools to store, view, and update student details easily, ensuring all records are accurate and well-structured.

Attendance Marking

The Attendance Marking feature provides a simple and efficient way for schools to record daily attendance. It allows teachers to mark students as present or absent quickly using a clean and easy-to-use interface.

Report Generation

The Report Generation feature allows schools to quickly create detailed attendance reports with ease. Instead of compiling data manually, the system automatically organizes attendance records and generates accurate reports within seconds.

Why go digital?

Digital attendance systems streamline record-keeping by replacing manual registers with secure, automated processes. They reduce errors, save time, and ensure data is safely stored and easily accessible. Additionally, they enable instant report generation, improve organization, and support informed decision-making
